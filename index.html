PROJECTS

Computer Mouse Body

Used Solidworks form tools to design an original sleek and ergonomic computer mouse body to be 3D printed. Constraints included designing around a circuit board from a seperate mouse and designing for 3D printability. The final result is the mouse I use to this day!

Conveyor Assembly

Used Solidworks and imported CAD models from McMaster-Carr to create a large modular conveyor assembly with sorting abilities. The conveyor was designed with the specifications of needing to transport 20x20x20cm and 10x10x30cm boxes with a max weight of 8.48kg.

Goose Escape Game

Used C++ and BearlibTerminal to program an turnbased endless roguelike dungeon game with randomly generated walls and enemies. The deeper you go the more enemies and walls you spawn you'll collect bird feed along the way which allow you to pause all enemy movement. How deep can you go before you are overwelmed by enemies?
