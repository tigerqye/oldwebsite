PROJECTS

Computer Mouse Design

Used Solidworks form tools to design an original sleek and ergonomic computer mouse shell to be 3D printed. Constraints included designing around a circuit board from a seperate mouse and designing for 3D printability. More details can be found here.

Conveyor CAD

Used Solidworks and imported CAD models from McMaster-Carr to create a large modular conveyor assembly with sorting abilities. The conveyor was designed with the specifications of needing to transport 20x20x20cm and 10x10x30cm boxes with a max weight of 8.48kg. More details can be found here.

Canada Airport

Designed an airport program that read airplane flights from a textfile and displayed departure and arrival flights to Toronto. More details can be found here.

Turntable

Used CAD knowlegde to design a turntable in Fusion 360 and printed it out using a 3D printer. Model was designed around integrating metal spheres from a children's toy. More details can be found here.
